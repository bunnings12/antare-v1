# Antares v0.1 – Minimum Viable Rescue Drone (Dec 2025 target)

## Hard requirements
- 3D-printable frame in <24 h on a Prusa/Ender
- Total cost per unit < $400 (without battery)
- Single unit lift ≥ 8 kg (4 kg payload + 4 kg vehicle)
- 3+ units must cooperatively lift 15 kg sandbag in tethered test
- Manual override + full autonomous mode

## Reference parts (starting point)
- Motors: iFlight XING2 3110 900KV
- ESCs: 4-in-1 60–80A BLHeli_32
- Props: 13–15 inch carbon
- Flight controller: SpeedyBee F7 Mini or Matek H743
- Battery: 6S 8000–12000 mAh Li-Ion

CAD drop within 48 hours. First test flight videos required before New Year’s Eve.
